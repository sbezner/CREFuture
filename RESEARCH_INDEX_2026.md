## Key Research Findings Summary

### What's Becoming Table Stakes (Next 12 Months)

| Technology | Current Status | Timeline to Table Stakes | Business Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lease Abstraction | Production | Now | $125K-225K/year (100 props) |
| Tenant Engagement AI | Scaling | Now-Q2 2026 | 15-20% conversion lift |
| Energy Optimization | Production | Now-Q4 2026 | $570K-1.45M/year (100 props) |
| Real-time Valuation | Rapid adoption | Q2-Q4 2026 | $100K-500K/year efficiency |
| Leasing Intelligence | Scaling | Q3 2026 | $600K-1.2M/year (100 props) |
| Portfolio Dashboards | Early production | Q4 2026 | 5-10% NOI improvement |

### Market Consolidation Signals

**Trane/BrainBox AI (January 2025)**
- Signal: Equipment manufacturer acquiring HVAC AI leader
- Implication: Bundling reduces friction, accelerates adoption
- For Buyers: Expect faster scaling, competitive matching from other HVAC vendors

**Yardi/Claude Integration (2025)**
- Signal: Enterprise PMS integrating with Claude
- Implication: Property data available to LLMs, enables custom AI agents
- For Buyers: Ecosystem advantage for Yardi users; others must build integrations

**CoreLogic → Cotality Rebrand (March 2025)**
- Signal: 100-year-old mortgage data company rebranding as AI company
- Implication: Incumbent players betting future on AI; aggressive R&D investment
- For Buyers: Data standardization (CLIP) enables better AI across ecosystem

**Proptech Funding Concentration ($16.7B in 2025)**
- Signal: 31 companies captured 72.3% of VC investment
- Implication: Capital flowing to proven, scalable platforms; seed-stage starvation
- For Buyers: Consolidation accelerating; smaller vendors will be acquired

### The Execution Gap (Most Important Finding)

**The Numbers**:
- 92% of CRE companies have AI pilots
- Only 5% have achieved program objectives
- 88% of investors have AI pilots
- 5% report achieving most objectives

**Why Pilots Fail at Scale**:
1. **Data infrastructure inadequate** (PRIMARY REASON - 60% of failures)
2. **Organizational resistance** (20% of failures)
3. **Governance/trust issues** (15% of failures)
4. **Technology limitations** (5% of failures)

**What Works**:
- Infrastructure-first approaches (consolidate data before selecting tools)
- Workflow embedding (AI within existing processes, not parallel systems)
- Clear governance (decision frameworks, human oversight, audit trails)
- Continuous measurement (quarterly performance review, model retraining)

### Investment & ROI Profile (100-Property Portfolio)

**Total 2025-2026 Investment**: $4M-$5M  
**Breakdown**:
- Lease abstraction: $50K-$100K (payback: 1-2 months)
- Energy optimization: $2M-$2.5M (payback: 3.4 years, ongoing benefit $570K-$1.45M/year)
- Leasing intelligence: $500K-$1M (payback: 1-2 years)
- Portfolio management: $1M-$2M (payback: 1-3 years)

**Annual Benefit Year 3+**: $1.7M-$4.65M  
**Blended ROI (Post-payback)**: 43-116% annually  
**Competitive Advantage**: 12-18 month head start on information asymmetry
