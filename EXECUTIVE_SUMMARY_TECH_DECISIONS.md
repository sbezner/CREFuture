## The Execution Gap Reality

**92%** of CRE companies have AI pilots  
**5%** have achieved their program objectives  
**KEY INSIGHT**: The problem isn't technology. It's execution.

**Why pilots fail at scale**:
1. Data infrastructure inadequate for production (PRIMARY REASON)
2. Organizational resistance and workflow challenges
3. Lack of governance and trust in AI-generated decisions

**What successful organizations do differently**:
- Consolidate data BEFORE selecting analytics tools
- Embed AI in existing workflows (not parallel systems)
- Define clear governance and decision frameworks
- Measure and iterate continuously

## Three Decision Paths

### Path 1: Fast Follower (Recommended)
**Timeline**: Implement 2025-2026  
**Investment**: $4M-$5M  
**Risk Level**: Moderate  
**Payback**: 12-24 months on initial pilots  
**Annual Benefit Year 3+**: $1.7M-$4.65M  

**Action**: Begin lease abstraction and tenant engagement pilots immediately. Establish data infrastructure in parallel. Scale to broader deployment in 2026.

### Path 2: Early Adopter
**Timeline**: Begin 2025, full implementation by 2027  
**Investment**: $5M-$7M  
**Risk Level**: Higher  
**Payback**: 18-36 months (includes learning curve)  
**Annual Benefit Year 3+**: $2M-$5M+ (with 12-18 month advantage)  

**Action**: Systematic pilots in 10-20% of portfolio. Build internal data and AI expertise. Position for market-timing and information-asymmetry advantages.

### Path 3: Wait and See
**Timeline**: 2027+  
**Risk Level**: Highest (permanent disadvantage)  
**Outcome**: Likely loses 15-25% competitive advantage to early movers  

**Not recommended**: Structural disadvantage compounds over time as information asymmetry widens.

## Key Questions for Your Investment Committee

### Question 1: Can we afford NOT to invest?
**Analysis**: Fast followers investing $4-5M in 2025-2026 generate $1.7M-$4.65M annual benefits within 3 years. Non-investors face permanent 15-25% competitive disadvantage in information quality and decision speed.

**Answer**: Opportunity cost of waiting exceeds cost of deployment.

### Question 2: What's our data readiness?
**Analysis**: Data infrastructure is the primary barrier. Successful organizations consolidate data BEFORE selecting analytics tools.

**Recommendation**: Conduct data audit immediately. This is the prerequisite for other decisions.

### Question 3: What's our risk tolerance on early adoption?
**Analysis**: Lease abstraction and tenant engagement are low-risk, high-ROI. Leasing intelligence is moderate-risk. Portfolio optimization requires data infrastructure readiness.

**Recommendation**: Stage implementation by risk level. Begin with low-risk, high-ROI applications. Scale to higher-risk applications as data infrastructure matures.

### Question 4: Who are our competitors doing?
**Analysis**: 88% have pilots. 5% have achieved objectives. Most competitors are stuck in the pilot phase. Early execution provides 12-18 month advantage.

**Recommendation**: Begin fast-follower path in 2025 to establish structure before competitors execute at scale in 2026-2027.

### Question 5: What happens if we're wrong?
**Analysis**: Technologies discussed are proven at scale. Risk is not technology failure but execution/organizational challenges. These are manageable with proper governance.

**Recommendation**: Begin with low-risk pilots. Measure results quarterly. Scale based on proven performance.
